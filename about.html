About Abe Books

Welcome to the World of Rare and Used Books

At AbeBooks, we are passionate about connecting book lovers with the stories and knowledge they seek. As one of the world's most trusted online marketplaces for new, used, rare, and out-of-print books, we've been on a mission to make literary treasures accessible to all since our inception.

Our History

Founded in 1995, AbeBooks has a rich history rooted in a love for literature. What began as a small group of independent booksellers coming together to reach a global audience has grown into a vast community of book enthusiasts. Over the years, we've expanded our offerings and partnered with booksellers from around the world to provide an unmatched selection of books, spanning genres, languages, and centuries.

Our Commitment

At the heart of AbeBooks is a commitment to book culture, exploration, and discovery. We believe that every book holds a unique story and that there's a reader for every book. Our platform is dedicated to preserving the joy of reading and the appreciation for printed words in an increasingly digital world.

Why Choose AbeBooks?

  • Unrivaled Selection: With millions of books from independent sellers, collectors, and libraries, AbeBooks is your gateway to finding that elusive title or cherished edition.
  • Trusted Sellers: Our global network of reputable booksellers ensures that every purchase is a reliable and secure transaction.
  • Passion for Books: We share your passion for books and are dedicated to fostering a community of readers, collectors, and enthusiasts.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We support sustainability efforts by giving new life to used books, reducing waste, and minimizing our carbon footprint.

Join Us on a Literary Journey

Whether you're a seasoned collector, a student searching for course materials, or simply a book lover exploring new realms, AbeBooks is here to accompany you on your literary journey. Dive into our vast catalog, uncover hidden gems, and connect with a world of stories waiting to be discovered.
